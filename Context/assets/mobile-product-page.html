About Product

What makes KOKO cold brew different?
Every cup is traceable to its source. We cold-steep for 18 hours minimum — longer than most brands — for a richer, smoother result.
How should I store it?
Keep refrigerated and consume within 2 weeks of opening. Best served over ice.
