Why Us

WE'RE OBSESSED WITH GETTING IT RIGHT.

Most cold brew is rushed. Diluted. Compromised. KOKO exists because we refused to accept that. Every decision we make — sourcing, steeping, bottling — starts with one question: is this the best it can be?

OUR STANDARDS

No Sugar Added

Clean energy, zero crash. Just the natural sweetness of properly extracted arabica.

72H Extraction

Time is the only ingredient you can't rush. 72 hours of cold steeping unlocks what heat destroys.

Single Origin

We know exactly which farm, which region, which harvest. Traceable to the source, every time.

Specialty Grade

Top 1% arabica only. Sourced from elevation farms. If it scores below 80, it doesn't make the cut.
